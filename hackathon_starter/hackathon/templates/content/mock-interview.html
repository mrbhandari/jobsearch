Record a mock interview

Use this tool hear what you sound like in an interview and share with a friend or mentor to get advice on how to make it better.

Interview Questions:

#1 Tell me about yourself.

#2 What are your strengths?

#3 What are your weaknesses?

#4 Why do you want this job?

#5 Where would you like to be in your career five years from now?

#6 What's your ideal company?

#6 What attracted you to this company?

#6 Why should we hire you?



Record Download
Instructions: Allow microphone access at the top of the screen.
Then, press the record button and answer the first question.
Continue by clicking the next question.
When you are done press "Record" again to stop recording.
You'll then be able to download a copy of your recording to listen to yourself or send to a friend for advice.
