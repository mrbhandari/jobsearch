How it Works

When you text us, what you can expect:

Specificity

Connect to precise expertise to gain complete understanding of any job or career path

Availability

Texting is available 24/7 so it works fast and is flexible to your schedule.

Interactivity

Texting enables us to have ongoing conversations that facilitate better knowledge exchange.

Trust

Texting is one-on-one and confidential, so you can feel comfortable asking any question to help you learn.

What can you text about?

Applying but not getting interviews? Fix your resume, cover letter, and application

Text someone who’s on the other side. See how your resume and cover letter stack up to other candidates’ they receive. See if there’s any obvious skill gaps or something missing in your strategy.

Getting the interview but not the job? Expert interview prep

We’ll match you with an expert - someone who has worked in the job or industry you are interviewing for. Together we’ll craft a list of questions you’ll likely face so you can be prepared for what is often the most nerve-wrecking parts of the job search: the interview.

Not sure where to start? Data driven opportunity matches

Have a counselor match you to opportunities based on your background, skills, and goals. If there’s a gap between what you want to do and your current skill set we’ll help you find the most common path to your ideal job. We’ll build a contact list and a list of events you should attend to get in touch with potential employers.
